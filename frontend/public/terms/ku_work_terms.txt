3) Privacy Notice (Student supplement)
----------------------------------------
This section supplements the KU Work Core Privacy Notice. Refer to the Core Notice for controller information, processors, international transfers, security, retention, and your rights.

Student/KU‑specific data categories (in addition to the Core categories):
- Identification and profile data: name, student ID, KU email, contact details, academic information (e.g., major, student status), and optional links (GitHub, LinkedIn).
- Uploaded content/files: profile photo and documents supporting your student status; information you submit for your profile or applications.
- Communications: service/transactional emails related to your account and submissions.

Student purposes and legal bases (supplementing the Core):
- Student account creation, verification, and access to student‑controlled features.
  Legal bases: Contract necessity (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)); PDPA: contractual necessity.
- Document and profile review (including AI‑assisted decision support with human oversight).
  Legal bases: Contract necessity and Legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)/(f)); PDPA: contractual necessity/legitimate interest.

Note: We do not conduct behavior‑tracking analytics (see Core Notice). Use of strictly necessary technical logs/cookies may be required for session management and security.
